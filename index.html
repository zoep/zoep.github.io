About me

I am a PhD student in the area of programming languages at Princeton University, working with Prof. Andrew Appel. I am currently working on CertiCoq and QuickChick projects. I obtained my Master’s degree from ENS Cachan (Master Parisien de Recherche en Informatique) and my diploma (5-year engineering degree) from National Technical University of Athens.
